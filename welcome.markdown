# Welcome to Full Stack
Welcome to Full Stack Web Development

In this unit, we take the fundamentals from first year Web and expand on them to learn how to develop fully functioning applications. The architectures and technologies taught are highly relevant in industry today and your assignment will serve as an excellent addition to your portfolio when going for placements/jobs.

**The Full Stack Web Development module is assessed by coursework only - one assignment worth 100% of the overall grade. You need to achieve a grade of at least 40% to pass the module. Hopefull, you will want to get a much higher mark. Remember, you need to pass all of your modules in order to progress into your final year.**
